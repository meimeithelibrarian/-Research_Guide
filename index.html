üîç Smart Search Strategies

Boolean Search Magic

Operator Function Example
"quotation marks" Exact phrases "social media"
AND Narrows search (both terms must appear) "social media" AND "mental health"
OR Broadens search (either term can appear) "teenagers" OR "adolescents"
NOT Excludes unwanted results "mental health" NOT "children"

üìù MLA Formatting Essentials

Paper Setup Checklist

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Spacing: Double-space everything
  • Margins: 1 inch all sides
  • Header: Last name + page number (top right)

First Page Info (Top Left)

Your Name
Professor's Name
Course Name
Date (Day Month Year)

Example: 10 September 2024
NOT: September 10, 2024

In-Text Citations Quick Reference

Situation Format Example
Basic quote (Author Page#) (Smith 45)
Author in sentence (Page#) Smith argues "..." (45)
Website/no pages (Author) (Johnson)
No author ("Short Title") ("Climate Report")

üìö Works Cited Format

Basic Pattern: Author. "Title." Container, Date, Location.

Common Examples:

Book:
Morrison, Toni. Beloved. Vintage Books, 1987.

Website Article:
Johnson, Mary. "Climate Solutions." Environmental News, 15 Oct. 2024, www.envnews.com/solutions.

Database Article:
Smith, John. "Student Success Strategies." Journal of Education, vol. 25, no. 3, 2024, pp. 45-67. Academic Search Complete.

Works Cited Page Rules:

  • Title: "Works Cited" (centered, no bold/italics)
  • Location: Last page of paper
  • Format: Double-spaced, hanging indent
  • Order: Alphabetical by author's last name

üìã Annotated Bibliography Made Simple

What Is It? Works Cited entries + short summaries (annotations) of each source

Three-Sentence Annotation Formula:
1. What does this source cover?
2. What are the main findings/arguments?
3. How will you use this in your research?

Sample Entry:

Johnson, Sarah. "Digital Detox for Students." Student Wellness Journal, vol. 15, no. 3, 2024, pp. 23-35. This study examines 200 college students who took week-long breaks from social media platforms. Johnson found that 78% of participants reported better sleep, improved focus, and reduced anxiety during their digital detox period. This source will provide concrete evidence for my argument that students should consider regular social media breaks.

üßä ICE Method for Quotes

I = Introduce

Who said it? What's their credibility?

C = Cite

Quote or paraphrase with proper citation

E = Explain

Why does this matter to your argument?

Example:

Introduce: Leading education researcher Dr. Smith conducted a study on student success.
Cite: She found that "students who start research early score 23% higher on papers" (Smith 78).
Explain: This demonstrates that time management directly impacts academic performance.
